RECEIVER SETUP

In this section, I will go over the setup of the hardware receivers that you will use to cast your media to. These receivers will connect to your TV via HDMI and will connect to your content wirelessly over your home network.

Chromecast

You can get the Chromecast at the Google Play Store

  1. Plug the HDMI cable into the TV HDMI port
  2. Connect the Chromecast to power
  3. Turn the TV on and change the input to the Chromecast you just plugged in
  4. Download the Chromecast app for your Android/Windows/iPhone
  5. Open the app and follow the instructions to connect your Chromecast to the internet
  6. Wait for Chromecast to finish setup


Apple TV

You can get the Apple TV at the Apple Website

  1. Plug the HDMI cable into the TV HDMI port
  2. Connect the Apple TV to power
  3. Make sure your iPhone is connected to your home WiFi
  4. Turn on your iPhone's bluetooth
  5. Wait for the Automatic Setup to show up
  6. Enter your Apple ID and password
  7. Choose preferences that are prompted on you iPhone's screen
  8. Wait for Apple TV to finish setup
