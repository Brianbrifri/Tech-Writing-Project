SUBSCRIPTIONS

Here I will cover the basics of the subscription services available. These will supplement your personal media collection at a monthly price.

Netflix

  • At $9.99/month, Netflix offers a wide range of Movies and TV Shows as a combination of well known movie studios as well as original programming

Amazon Prime Instant Video

  • Many people have an Amazon Prime subscription; but what many of those subscribers are not aware of is the library of movies and TV Shows incuded with that subscription. Amazon Prime Instant Video offers a lot of HBO shows that have been out for a while as well as recent blockbuster movies

HULU

Hulu offers both a paid and free tier

  • Paid Tier
    • Offers TV Shows the day after they air on Paid (or local) TV
    • Offers limited and no commercials options for $7.99 and 11.99/mo respectively
    • Ability to watch on mobile devices as well

  • Free Tier
    • Commercials
    • No access to current TV shows
    • Can only watch on computer

HBO Now

  • At $14.99/month, HBO Now delivers all of HBO without being tied to a Pay TV provider
  • You get access to ALL of HBO's shows as well as being able to view episodes live as they air
  • It provides an extensive library of movies as well

YouTube

  • YouTube has recently launched a movie section that offers free to the public classic movies
  • In addition to these movies, YouTube is a leading provider in entertainment media that is essential to every home entertainment system

Showtime

  • Showtime is another Pay TV channel that has branched off their traditionally Pay TV exclusive content to the internet
  • Here you will have access to all of Showtime's live and archived content. You can get it standalone or as an add on to Hulu for $7.99/month
